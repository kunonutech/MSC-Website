About Mang'oto Scholars Community

We are a growing scholars community from Mang'oto Ward,

from Makete district in Njombe, Tanzania.
Most of us are first-generation university scholars,
hence, we feel obliged to give back to our community.
We believe that the small acts of kindness we show to our
community will create a hopeful and prosperous community to future generations.
